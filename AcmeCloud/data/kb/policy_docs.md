# Claims Submission Policy

Members should submit claims within 90 days from the date of service.
Late submissions may require manager review and supporting documentation.

# Identity Verification Policy

Before any account profile update (address, insurance, phone),
AcmeCloud support must verify member identity with at least two factors.

# Escalation Rules

Clinical symptoms, emergencies, or medical diagnosis requests must be routed
to licensed care staff. Support agent must not provide diagnosis.
